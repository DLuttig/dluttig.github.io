Neuroplasticity and Growth Mindset

Neuroplasticity

Neuroplasticity plays a roll in people ability to learning, research, and practice new skills. Example of these skill are typing, speaking a new language, reading, etc. As you practice a new skill you brain forges new neuron paths, and becomes stronger as you continue practicing.

Neuroplasticity has far-reaching implications and possibilities for almost every aspect of human life and culture from education to medicine. Its limits are not yet known. What is know is that we can improve your neuroplasticity development by practicing multiple skills

  • Get enough magnesium
  • Get enough quality sleep
  • Eating fish ( Omega-3)
  • Exercise hard (My preferred HIIT)
  • Intermitted fating
  • Learn an instrument
